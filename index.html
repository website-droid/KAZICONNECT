Our Story

We're AjiraPrime

Founded in 2024, AjiraPrime was born from a simple observation: Kenya has immense talent, but finding the right professional for any job — from plumbing to legal advice — was still a challenge. We set out to build a platform that connects every Kenyan to the right professional, wherever they are.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of clients and professionals across all 47 counties. Our mission is to make Kenya's gig economy transparent, trustworthy, and accessible to everyone.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AjiraPrime.

How does AjiraPrime make money?

We charge a small commission on completed jobs (5-10%), offer premium subscriptions for professionals, featured listings, urgent job fees, instant withdrawal fees, and advertising.

How do I create an account?

Click "Get Started" or "Sign Up" in the top right corner. You can sign up as a client or a professional. Fill in your details and you're ready to go!

How does M-Pesa escrow work?

You pay the full amount upfront into our secure M-Pesa escrow account. Funds are held safely until you confirm the job is complete and you're satisfied.

Are professionals verified?

Yes! Every professional on AjiraPrime goes through a thorough verification process including ID checks, credential validation, and portfolio reviews.

Can I hire across counties?

Absolutely! You can hire professionals in your county or any of the 47 counties. For cross-county jobs, the app calculates travel costs automatically.

What fees do you charge?

We charge a transparent platform fee. It's 0% for your first 10 jobs, then 5% for verified pros, and 3% for premium members. Clients pay no platform fees.

What if I'm not satisfied?

We have a dispute resolution process. If you're not happy with the work, you can report the issue and our team will mediate and issue a refund if appropriate.

How long does payment take?

Once you mark a job as complete, funds are released to the professional within 24 hours. They can withdraw instantly to their M-Pesa account.

Is there a mobile app?

Yes! We have apps for both Android and iOS. Download them from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store for the best experience.

How do I get featured?

Professionals can upgrade to Premium or purchase Featured Listings directly from their dashboard to appear at the top of search results.

Safety

Safety Tips

Your safety is our top priority. Here's how to stay safe on AjiraPrime.

For Clients

  • Always check the professional's rating and reviews before hiring
  • Look for the "Verified" badge on professional profiles
  • Communicate only through the AjiraPrime chat for record-keeping
  • Never pay outside the app — use M-Pesa escrow for security
  • Share your location with a friend or family member when meeting a pro

For Professionals

  • Ensure your profile is complete with verification documents
  • Confirm job details with the client before traveling
  • Ask for clear instructions and photos of the job site
  • Trust your instincts — if something feels off, don't proceed
  • Report any suspicious behavior to AjiraPrime immediately

Emergency Contacts

  • AjiraPrime Support: 0717 883 986
  • Police Emergency: 999 or 112
  • Gender Violence Hotline: 1195
